    ABOUT US

    The Venkateswar School

    Venkateswar School is an unique institution offering holistic and value based education to its pupils to support innovation and leadership in the child.

    Teaching in our school is given through qualified, committed and experienced teachers. Besides teaching, extra-curricular activities are given prime importance in our school in order to promote the inner-potentiality and all
    round development of the student.

    It imparts high quality education in English Medium as per C.B.S.E. Syllabys to thestudents and gets the proud privilege of being a leading educational institution of the State.

    Show More

    The Venkateswar School is run by the VEMS trust, having a group of Schools in and around Bhubaneswar.

    It imparts high quality education in English Medium as per C.B.S.E. Syllabys to the students and gets the proud privilege of being a leading educational institution of the State.

    Courses of studies are according to the syllabus prescribed by the VEMS trust, Bhubaneswar as per the latest guidelines and the syllabi issued by the N.C.E.R.T. New Delhi.

    Why Venkateswar School ?

    Besides teaching, extra-curricular activities are given prime importance in our school in order to promote the inner-potentiality and all-round development of the student.

    Moral Education and yoga training is given to all students with a view to make them disciplined and sociable future citizens of the Country.

    School have a strong program of core academic subjects such as English, history, mathematics, science, arts?

    For Parents

    Parents should check the School Diary Daily.

    Parents should ensure the functionality of their child.

    Parents should acknowledge the late attendance in the school diary every month.

    Parents should observe their child uniform.

    Parents and guardians are requested to extend their whole hearted co-operation for the smooth running of the school and proper education of their child/ ward.

    .

    Mandatory Information

    Computer training is also given to our students from lower classes on extra payments.

    The timing will be from 8.00 A.M. to 12.00 Noon. Students should reach the school by 7.50 A.M. as the school gate will be closed at 8.00 A.M. sharp.

    As per the CBSE norms we have adopted Continuous and comprehensive evaluation (CCE) system for assessment.

    75% of attendance on working days of the school in a year is compulsory.
